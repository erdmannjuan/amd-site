Automation Training Services | Robot & PLC Training | AMD Machines

Professional automation training: robot operation, programming, PLC, vision systems and maintenance for operators and engineers.

Maximize your automation investment by developing your team's skills. AMD Machines offers comprehensive training programs designed to help operators, technicians, and engineers work effectively with automated systems. Our practical, hands-on approach ensures your team can confidently operate, maintain, and optimize your equipment.

Training Programs

Operator Training

Empower your operators to run automated systems safely and efficiently:

Basic Operation - System startup and shutdown procedures - Normal operating sequences - Part loading and unloading - Basic HMI navigation - Safety procedures and emergency stops

Advanced Operation - Program selection and changeovers - Parameter adjustments - Quality monitoring and inspection - Minor fault recovery - Production data logging

Maintenance Training

Develop in-house maintenance capabilities:

Preventive Maintenance - Scheduled maintenance procedures - Lubrication and inspection routines - Wear item replacement - Calibration verification - Documentation requirements

Troubleshooting - Systematic fault diagnosis - Electrical troubleshooting - Mechanical problem solving - Using diagnostic tools - Reading error codes and logs

Repair Skills - Component replacement procedures - Servo motor and drive service - Pneumatic system maintenance - Sensor replacement and setup - Safety system testing

Programming Training

Build programming expertise within your organization:

Basic Programming - Program structure fundamentals - Motion commands and paths - I/O programming basics - Simple modifications and edits - Backup and restore procedures

Advanced Programming - Complex logic and decision making - Vision system integration - Communication protocols - Custom HMI development - Multi-robot coordination

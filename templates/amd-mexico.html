Mexico's Leading Automation Partner

AMD is a market leader in automation and robotic systems in Mexico. From our three facilities in Querétaro, Central Mexico, we design, build, and support custom automated machines for manufacturers across the country — backed by more than 30 years of experience and over 2,500 machines delivered.

We are the first choice for many manufacturers in the region who need reliable, high-performance automation that's engineered locally and supported locally.

Our Mexico Presence

Three Manufacturing Facilities in Querétaro — Our 70,000+ sq ft of combined floor space houses everything from engineering and design through final assembly and testing. Every system is built and proven in-house before it reaches your production floor.

Sales Office in Chicago — For customers coordinating cross-border projects, our Chicago office provides local support across North America.

New Texas Service Facility — We're expanding our U.S. footprint with a service facility in Texas, bringing maintenance and support closer to customers on both sides of the border.

Why Manufacturers in Mexico Choose AMD

Local Engineering, Local Support — Our team is based in Querétaro, one of Mexico's most important manufacturing corridors. We understand the local supply chain, labor market, and regulatory environment. When you need support, we're hours away — not days.

Proven Track Record — With 2,500+ machines delivered across automotive, medical, electronics, aerospace, and consumer products, we've earned the trust of Tier 1 suppliers and OEMs operating in Mexico.

End-to-End Capability — From concept and design through manufacturing, integration, installation, and ongoing maintenance and support, we handle every phase of your automation project. No handoffs, no finger-pointing.

Cross-Border Expertise — With facilities in Mexico and the U.S., we support manufacturers who operate on both sides of the border. Our bilingual engineering team makes collaboration seamless.

Application-Specific Solutions

Servo Press Applications

From bearing press-fits to complex forming operations, we build servo press systems for virtually any pressing application.

Servo Press for Press-Fit Assembly

The most common application we see. When you're pressing bearings, bushings, seals, or pins into housings, you need force control and verification - not just brute force.

Bearing Press-Fit

Servo press for bearing press fit applications requires careful control of insertion force and final position. We monitor the force-displacement curve to detect undersized bores, contamination, or misaligned parts before they become scrap.

Bushing Insertion

Servo press for bushing insertion typically needs variable force profiles - gentle start to find the bore, controlled insertion, then verification of final seat. We've done thousands of these across automotive and appliance.

Shaft Insertion

Servo press for shaft insertion into motor rotors, gear assemblies, or bearing races. Critical for electric motor assembly where rotor-stator alignment matters.

Pin & Insert Pressing

Servo press for pin insertion and insert pressing. From dowel pins to threaded inserts, we build press fit servo press systems that verify every insertion.

Interference Fit Expertise: When you're pressing parts with tight interference fits, the force-displacement curve tells the whole story. Our servo press for interference fit applications captures and analyzes every curve in real-time.

Servo Press for Crimping, Staking & Riveting

Crimping and staking operations need controlled deformation - too little and the joint is weak, too much and you've damaged the component. Servo presses excel here.

Crimping Stations

Our servo press crimping station designs handle electrical terminals, hose fittings, tube assemblies, and mechanical fasteners. The servo press for crimping application monitors crimp force and height for 100% verification.

Staking Operations

Electric servo press for staking operations - orbital staking, radial staking, and spin staking. We integrate the right tooling and monitoring for your specific joint requirements.

Riveting

Servo press for riveting applications including self-piercing rivets and conventional rivets. Some applications call for orbital riveting servo press integration which we handle as well.

Swaging

Servo press for swaging tube ends, cable terminals, and other components requiring controlled radial compression.

Servo Press for Forming, Embossing & Punching

Beyond assembly, servo presses are excellent for controlled forming operations where you need precise force and position control.

Metal Forming

Servo press for metal forming where you need controlled velocity profiles through the forming stroke. Programmable deceleration reduces shock loading on tooling.

Embossing

Servo press for embossing operations requiring precise depth control and consistent force application across the part surface.

Punching Stations

Servo press for punching station applications. The controlled breakthrough reduces shock and extends tool life compared to mechanical presses.

Forming Cell Automation

Complete servo press forming cell automation including part handling, press operation, and quality verification.

Quality Assurance

Force-Displacement Monitoring

This is really where servo presses shine. Every press operation generates a force-displacement curve that tells you exactly what happened during the press.

Force Displacement Monitoring Servo Press

Every servo press with force monitoring we integrate captures the complete force-position relationship throughout the stroke. This curve is compared against acceptance windows in real-time - bad parts get rejected before they ever leave the station.

A servo press with displacement monitoring tracks position to micron-level accuracy. Combined with force data, you get the complete picture of what's happening between your parts.

Press Fit Force Displacement Curve Analysis

The press fit force displacement curve reveals everything about your press operation:

  • Initial contact point - detects part presence and alignment
  • Insertion slope - reveals interference fit and surface condition
  • Peak force - confirms proper seating depth
  • Final position - verifies assembly dimension

Our press force signature analysis compares each curve against known-good reference envelopes. Parts outside the window get flagged immediately.

In-Process Quality Monitoring

In process quality monitoring for press fit operations means you catch problems as they happen - not downstream at final inspection. A poka yoke servo press station physically prevents bad parts from continuing down the line.

Data & Traceability

Every servo press with traceability we build logs complete curve data for each assembly:

  • Part serial number linkage
  • Complete force-position curves
  • Pass/fail status with failure codes
  • Timestamp and operator ID
  • Recipe and parameter settings

Our servo press data logging and reporting systems integrate with your MES, quality database, or standalone storage.

Press Monitoring Systems

Replace Pneumatic Press with Servo Press

If your current pneumatic presses don't give you the force control or data you need, replacing them with servo makes sense. Our servo press replacement for pneumatic press projects typically include:

  • Removal of existing cylinder and valving
  • Installation of servo press actuator in existing frame (when possible)

  • Updated safety system to current standards
  • Operator training and documentation

Add Force Monitoring to Existing Press

Sometimes you don't need a full servo press - you just need to add force monitoring to existing equipment. Our servo press upgrade force monitoring packages add load cells, displacement sensors, and monitoring systems to your current presses.

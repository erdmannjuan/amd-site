## Rationale

### Title tag change
- **Old:** `Automated Assembly Machines Selection Guide`
- **New:** `Custom Automated Assembly Machines | Design, Build & Integration`
- **Why:** SERP competitors are manufacturer capability pages (Tri-Mation, PAR, JR Automation). "Selection Guide" signals educational/comparison intent, which doesn't match what searchers want — they want a builder. The new title positions AMD as a manufacturer ("Design, Build & Integration") and front-loads the primary keyword "Automated Assembly Machines." Stays under 60 characters (59 chars).

### Meta description change
- **Old:** `How to choose the right automated assembly machine. Compare rotary dial, linear transfer & robotic systems. Real ROI data from 2,500+ installations.`
- **New:** `We design and build custom automated assembly machines — rotary indexing, linear transfer, and robotic systems. 30 years, 2,500+ machines delivered. Get a quote.`
- **Why:** Shifts from guide framing ("How to choose") to manufacturer framing ("We design and build"). Includes the primary keyword, names the three machine types for relevance, adds authority signals (30 years, 2,500+ machines), and ends with a CTA. 155 characters.

### Category change
- **Old:** `Trends`
- **New:** `Assembly`
- **Why:** The post is about assembly machines — categorizing it under "Trends" sends the wrong topical signal. "Assembly" aligns with the keyword cluster and matches the site's existing category taxonomy (confirmed in other blog posts).

### Keywords change
- **Old:** `automated assembly machines, assembly automation, automatic assembly equipment, robotic assembly, assembly line automation, automated manufacturing assembly, assembly machine benefits`
- **New:** `automated assembly machines, automated assembly machinery, automated assembly systems, automated assembly equipment, assembly automation`
- **Why:** Replaces low-impression generic terms with the exact GSC queries driving impressions. "Automated assembly machinery" (1,022 impressions, position 4.9) and "automated assembly systems" (131 impressions, position 13.5) were missing entirely. Drops filler keywords ("assembly machine benefits") that have no search volume. Keeps "assembly automation" but it's listed last since `/solutions/assembly/` owns that cluster — this page uses it sparingly per cannibalization rules.

### H1 change
- **Old:** `Automated Assembly Machines Selection Guide` (derived from title field)
- **New:** `Automated Assembly Machines: Custom Systems Built for Your Process`
- **Why:** Retains the primary keyword "Automated Assembly Machines" in leading position. Drops "Selection Guide" (educational framing) and replaces with "Custom Systems Built for Your Process" (manufacturer framing that matches SERP intent). The colon structure keeps it scannable. Differentiates from the title tag to avoid redundancy while reinforcing the same keyword cluster.

### Fields kept unchanged
- `template`, `author`, `author_title`, `date`, `read_time`, `related_posts` — no reason to change these. The date stays at original publication to preserve any age-based ranking signals.

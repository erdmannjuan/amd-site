## Our Story

AMD Automation was founded over 25 years ago with a clear mission: build custom automated machines that solve real manufacturing problems. What began as a small engineering shop has grown into a full-service automation integrator, but our founding principles remain unchanged.

We've earned our reputation one project at a time, delivering reliable automation systems that perform as promised. Our customers return to us again and again because they know we'll solve their problems thoughtfully, build their equipment carefully, and stand behind our work completely.

Automated packaging systems handle your products from production through shipping-ready pallets. AMD Automation designs packaging lines that maintain your production throughput with reliable cartoning, case packing, and [palletizing](/solutions/palletizing/) systems.

## Packaging Technologies

### Cartoning

Primary packaging into cartons:

- Horizontal and vertical cartoners
- Tuck, glue, and lock bottom styles
- Robotic and mechanical loading
- Integrated printing and coding

### Case Packing

Secondary packaging for shipping:

- Top load and side load packers
- Wrap-around case formers
- Partition inserters
- Robotic case packing

### Tray Packing

For retail and display:

- Tray erecting and loading
- Shrink bundling
- Display-ready packaging
- Variety pack assembly

### Wrapping Systems

Protection for storage and transport:

- Stretch wrapping for pallets
- Shrink wrapping for products
- Flow wrapping for individual items
- Banding and strapping

## System Components

Complete packaging line solutions:

- **Product handling** - Conveyors, accumulators, and buffers
- **[Vision systems](/solutions/machine-vision/)** - Inspection and guidance
- **[Robotic cells](/solutions/robotic-cells/)** - Flexible pick and place
- **[Palletizing](/solutions/palletizing/)** - End-of-line stacking

## Changeover and Flexibility

Accommodate product variety:

- **Tool-less changeover** - Minimize downtime between products
- **Recipe management** - Store and recall package configurations
- **Mixed case packing** - Build rainbow pallets
- **SKU flexibility** - Handle product variety efficiently

## Line Integration

Packaging connects with your operations:

- **Upstream** - Synchronize with production equipment
- **Quality gates** - Reject defective products
- **[Marking systems](/solutions/marking-traceability/)** - Apply codes and labels
- **Warehouse** - Interface with WMS for shipping

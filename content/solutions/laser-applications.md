Laser processing provides non-contact precision for cutting, welding, marking, and surface treatment applications. AMD Machines integrates laser systems with [robotic cells](/solutions/robotic-cells/) and gantry motion for complete automated solutions.

## Laser Technologies

### Fiber Lasers

Ideal for metal processing:

- High efficiency and low maintenance
- Excellent beam quality for fine features
- 1-20+ kW for cutting and welding
- Fast response for high-speed applications

### CO2 Lasers

For non-metals and organic materials:

- Plastics, wood, textiles, paper
- Engraving and cutting
- Large format processing
- Established, reliable technology

### UV and Green Lasers

For sensitive materials:

- Cold ablation with minimal heat
- Electronics and medical applications
- Glass and ceramic processing
- Fine marking on plastics

## Laser Processes

### Cutting

- Metals: steel, aluminum, copper, titanium
- Non-metals: plastics, composites, fabrics
- 2D and 3D contour cutting
- Integration with [material handling](/solutions/material-handling/)

### Welding

- Keyhole and conduction mode welding
- Hermetic sealing for medical devices
- Battery and electronics assembly
- Complementary to [resistance welding](/solutions/welding/)

### Marking

- Part serialization and traceability
- 2D codes, barcodes, human-readable text
- Logos and decorative marking
- Integration with [machine vision](/solutions/machine-vision/) verification

Consumer products manufacturing requires automation that can handle product variety, seasonal demand swings, and constant pressure to reduce costs. AMD Automation builds flexible systems that meet these challenges.

## Flexible Manufacturing

Consumer products manufacturers often produce many SKUs on shared equipment. Our automation solutions feature:

- Quick changeover tooling and fixtures
- Recipe-based control systems
- Modular designs that adapt to new products
- Vision guidance that reduces hard tooling

## High-Speed Production

Consumer products demand efficient production. We design systems that maximize throughput:

- Continuous motion assembly machines
- High-speed pick-and-place systems
- Automated packaging and case packing
- Efficient material staging and feeding

## Product Protection

Consumer products must look perfect. Our material handling and automation protects product appearance:

- Gentle handling mechanisms
- Surface protection features
- Cosmetic inspection systems
- Proper orientation and staging

## Packaging Integration

End-of-line packaging is critical for consumer products. We integrate:

- Cartoning and case packing
- Shrink wrapping and bundling
- Labeling and coding
- Palletizing and stretch wrapping

## Applications

Our consumer products experience includes:

- Small appliances and housewares
- Personal care products
- Hardware and tools
- Sporting goods
- Home improvement products
- Pet products

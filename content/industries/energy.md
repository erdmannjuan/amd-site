The energy sector demands automation that handles large components, maintains strict quality standards, and adapts to rapidly evolving technologies. AMD Machines provides solutions for battery manufacturing, renewable energy equipment, and traditional power generation components.

## Battery Manufacturing Automation

Electric vehicle and grid storage batteries require precise, high-volume assembly:

- **Cell handling and sorting** - [Material handling](/solutions/material-handling/) for cylindrical, prismatic, and pouch cells
- **Module welding** - [Laser](/solutions/laser-applications/) and resistance [welding](/solutions/welding/) for cell interconnects
- **[Testing](/solutions/test-systems/)** - Electrical testing, leak detection, and thermal cycling
- **Pack assembly** - [Robotic cells](/solutions/robotic-cells/) for final battery pack assembly

## Solar Panel Manufacturing

Photovoltaic manufacturing automation includes:

- Cell handling and inspection with [machine vision](/solutions/machine-vision/)
- Automated stringing and tabbing
- Lamination and framing systems
- End-of-line testing and [palletizing](/solutions/palletizing/)

## Wind Energy Components

Large-scale manufacturing for wind turbines:

- Tower section welding and assembly
- Generator and gearbox assembly
- Blade manufacturing support
- Nacelle component assembly

## Power Generation Equipment

Traditional and renewable power equipment:

- Transformer coil winding and assembly
- Switchgear and panel assembly
- Generator component manufacturing
- Turbine component automation

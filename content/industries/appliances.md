Appliance manufacturing combines high-volume production with product variety, requiring automation that balances efficiency with flexibility. AMD Automation provides solutions for major appliance, HVAC, and white goods manufacturers.

## Cabinet and Structure Assembly

Automated assembly for appliance frames and enclosures:

- [Resistance welding](/solutions/welding/) for steel cabinet construction
- [Robotic handling](/solutions/robotic-cells/) for large panels
- Automated fastening with [torque-controlled screwdriving](/solutions/screwdriving/)
- [Adhesive dispensing](/solutions/dispensing/) for panel bonding

## Component Assembly

Subassembly automation for appliance systems:

- Motor and compressor assembly
- Valve and manifold assembly with [servo pressing](/solutions/servo-pressing/)
- Control panel and electronics assembly
- Hose and tubing connection

## Sealed System Testing

Critical testing for refrigeration and HVAC:

- [Leak testing](/solutions/test-systems/) for refrigerant circuits
- Pressure decay and helium leak detection
- Electrical and functional testing
- Run-in and performance verification

## Final Assembly and Packaging

End-of-line automation:

- Door and panel installation
- [Vision verification](/solutions/machine-vision/) for assembly completeness
- Functional testing before packaging
- [Palletizing](/solutions/palletizing/) and load-out

## Thermal Joining

Plastic component assembly:

- [Ultrasonic welding](/solutions/thermal-processing/) for detergent dispensers and reservoirs
- [Hot plate welding](/solutions/thermal-processing/) for large tanks and ducts
- Vibration welding for interior components

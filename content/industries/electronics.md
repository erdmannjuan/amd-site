Electronics manufacturing demands precision, cleanliness, and comprehensive testing. AMD Automation builds systems that handle sensitive electronic assemblies while ensuring quality at every step.

## ESD-Safe Design

Electrostatic discharge can damage sensitive electronic components. Our automation incorporates:

- ESD-safe materials throughout
- Proper grounding and bonding
- Ionization where needed
- ESD monitoring and compliance verification

## PCB Handling

Printed circuit board handling requires care:

- Edge handling to protect components
- Accurate positioning for downstream operations
- Board warpage accommodation
- Traceability marking integration

## Test Automation

We build comprehensive test systems including:

- In-circuit testing integration
- Functional test fixturing
- Boundary scan systems
- RF testing enclosures
- Programming and verification

## Conformal Coating

For products requiring environmental protection:

- Selective robotic coating application
- UV curing systems
- Coating inspection
- Masking automation

## Box Build

Final assembly automation for electronic products:

- Wire harness installation
- Fastening and securing
- Final testing and calibration
- Packaging and labeling

- Automotive electronics

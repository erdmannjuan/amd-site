Explore common automation applications designed and built by AMD Machines for high-volume manufacturing environments. Our systems are engineered around each customer's part geometry, process requirements, cycle time, quality standards, and production goals.

## Assembly & Joining

- **[Custom Automated Assembly Machines](/solutions/assembly/)** — turnkey assembly cells and lines built around your part and target cycle time.
- **[Servo Press Assembly Stations](/solutions/servo-pressing/)** — force- and position-controlled pressing for repeatable press-fit assembly.
- **[Robotic Screwdriving Systems](/solutions/screwdriving/)** — automated fastening with torque and depth verification.
- **[Robotic Welding Cells](/solutions/welding/)** — MIG, TIG, spot, and laser welding cells with positioners and fixturing.
- **[Automated Dispensing Systems](/solutions/dispensing/)** — adhesive, sealant, and fluid dispensing with closed-loop control.

## Test & Inspection

- **[Automated Leak Test Stations](/solutions/test-systems/)** — pressure-decay, mass-flow, and helium leak testing.
- **[End-of-Line Test Systems](/solutions/test-systems/)** — integrated end-of-line verification before product ships.
- **[Automated Functional Test Stations](/solutions/test-systems/)** — application-specific functional testing with full data capture.
- **[Machine Vision Inspection Stations](/solutions/machine-vision/)** — vision-guided inspection, measurement, and defect detection.
- **[Part Marking and Traceability Systems](/solutions/marking-traceability/)** — laser marking, 2D codes, and serialized traceability.

## Robotics & Material Handling

- **[Robotic Machine Tending Cells](/solutions/machine-tending/)** — CNC, press, and injection-molding tending.
- **[Robotic Bin Picking Systems](/solutions/bin-picking/)** — 3D vision-guided random bin picking.
- **[Automated Pick-and-Place Systems](/solutions/material-handling/)** — high-speed part transfer and placement.
- **[Robotic Palletizing Cells](/solutions/palletizing/)** — end-of-line stacking and unstacking.

## Finishing

- **[Robotic Deburring and Grinding Cells](/solutions/deburring/)** — automated edge and surface finishing.
- **[Robotic Polishing and Buffing Cells](/solutions/grinding-polishing/)** — consistent, repeatable surface finishing automation.
